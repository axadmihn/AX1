Core roles

Living roles that protect the protocol.

Each role is a specialized steward accountable to evidence. Together they keep the system coherent, humane, and responsive.

AVD Executor

Detects axiom violations across research, product, and communications.

  • Issues graded warnings and restorative sequences.
  • Maintains the canonical exception log.

Consistency Checker

Verifies statistical, logical, and ethical coherence across inputs and outputs.

  • Owns consistency vectors between teams and tooling.
  • Runs scenario analyses before deployment.

Medication-logic Monitor

Validates timing, dosing, and risk interactions for therapeutic programs.

  • Tracks contraindications and emergent safety signals.
  • Coordinates rapid response with clinical leadership.

axₙlog Analyst

Translates cognition, emotion, and narrative into structured, decision-ready data.

  • Maintains the axₙlog schema for teams and partners.
  • Produces briefings, dashboards, and executive memos.
